— Hero project · the one I'd lead an interview with

Hierarchical ACO-Firefly Hybridization — for genomic feature selection

2024 · Applied metaheuristics · breast-cancer oncology data · peer-reviewed paper

High-dimensional oncology datasets have thousands of features and dozens of samples. Standard ML feature-selection methods overfit, miss interactions, and produce unstable signatures that don't generalize across patient cohorts — exactly the wrong properties for clinical decision support.

We hybridized Ant Colony Optimization with the Firefly Algorithm in a hierarchical schema: ACO handles coarse subset discovery via pheromone-trail consensus, Firefly refines the inner selection via attraction-based intensification. The hierarchy is the contribution — neither algorithm alone produces stable signatures at this dimensionality.

82% — feature reduction
+14% — accuracy lift
peer — reviewed, 2024

№ 02 · 2024 — present

Fin — a currency math engine, dressed as a personal finance tool.

~600 daily users, zero floating-point bugs in 8 months of production. The interesting part is what's underneath the React.

I built Fin because every FX-rate app I tried silently lost cents to IEEE 754 rounding — which is fine for vibes, fatal for compounding. The currency engine is a small decimal-arithmetic kernel with rate-history snapshotting and reproducible conversion paths. Everything else is React.

~600daily users · 0FP bugs since launch
